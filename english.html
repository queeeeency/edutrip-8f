Malioboro is a famous street offering fun and experiences, colorful markets along the street, and lively energy with a calming moment. In Malioboro, I was strolling around with friends, encountering something exciting in every corner. Local handicrafts, traditional transportation, and overall the culture is what makes this street special and worth visiting. Additionally, the cool night breeze gently brushing past makes exploring even more enjoyable.

There are a lot of colorful markets catching our attention. There is a shop of randomly unique kid toys, full of colors with every different type: toy pistols, princess toys, and other toys that are able to fly. The shopkeeper is nice that we even chat and joke with him, turning a stop into a moment full of joy. Surrounded by all the lively stalls, our main focus is on finding the iconic I Love Jogja shirt. We check every shop full of racks of shirts, as each shopkeeper says they don't have what we are looking for. Eventually, we do find one but the color isn't quite right, so we decide not to get it. We then stop by a simple shop selling brownies, stacked neatly with every different variant. My eyes stop at the chocolate brownies - even from a distance, I could already taste a fudgy dessert bar with its rich chocolate flavor, appealing to the stomach. Then, I decide to buy some. I think it is nice to share a little piece of Malioboro with my family. Lastly, I try the famous Malio Gelato! Entering the place, the warm atmosphere fills the room. There are a lot of flavors: red velvet, cookies and cream, mint chocolate, and many other interesting flavors. I choose 2 flavors which are chocolate and cookies and cream. When I try it, it tastes like genuine chocolate! The flavors melts in my mouth, and it feels like heaven, definitely recommended! And by that, there truly are colorful markets that leave impressions.

Malioboro is a place with a good mix of lively and calm. Wandering through the street at night felt alive, as I had my friends around me. Their street's soft glow made every step feel even lighter.
